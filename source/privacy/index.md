# Privacy Policy

## Information Collection

TShot does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

## Screen Recording Permission

TShot requires Screen Recording permission solely to capture screenshots requested by the user.

Captured content is processed locally on the device and is never transmitted to external servers.

## Data Storage

Screenshots are stored locally on the user's device at locations chosen by the user.

## Third-Party Services

TShot does not use analytics services, advertising SDKs, tracking technologies, or cloud storage services.
